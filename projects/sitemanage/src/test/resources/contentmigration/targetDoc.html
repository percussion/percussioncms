Meyer Products Path Pro ATV Snowplow ? 50in., Model# 29000

Description

This Meyer Products Path Pro ATV Snowplow is a heavy-duty commercial plow that mounts to ATVs to clear snow from sidewalks or other tight areas not suitable for a truck. U.S.A.

  • Designed with flared wings to scoop and funnel snow faster and farther
  • 65-degree attack angle scrapes the surface more cleanly than standard straight blades
  • Self-Angling feature automatically angles the blade in the direction where most snow is gathering, eliminating the need to get off ATV
  • Locks in one of five different positions
  • Pre-drilled holes for easily adding snow deflector
  • Universal mount fits most ATVs on the market
  • Made of 14-gauge, high-tensile steel
