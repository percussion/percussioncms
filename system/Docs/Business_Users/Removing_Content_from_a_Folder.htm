Removing Content from a Folder

When you remove a Content Item from a folder, you remove the link to the Content Item, but you do not delete the Content Item from Rhythmyx. To remove a Content Item from Rhythmyx, you must have access to the purge action.

To remove content from a folder:

  1. Click the folder in the Navigation pane to display its contents in the Display pane.
  2. Click the Content Item in the Display Pane and right-click in the Display pane.

    Rhythmyx displays an Action Menu.

  3. In the Action Menu, choose Remove from Folder.

    Note: If any of the Content Items you select are involved in a cross-site link (either have an Active Assembly Relationship that links to a Content Item in a different Site managed in Rhythmyx, or is the target of such an Active Assembly Relationship), they will be skipped by the Remove from Folder action. To remove Content Items involved in a cross-site link, you must use the Force Remove from Folder action.

    Rhythmyx removes the Content Item link from the folder. The item still exists in the system, and may still exist in other folders.
