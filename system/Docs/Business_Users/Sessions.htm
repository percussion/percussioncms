Sessions

After you log in to Rhythmyx, you have entered a user session. You remain in that session until you log out or the system logs you out. Once you are logged out, you cannot return to your previous session; you must log in again and begin a new session.

Each session takes your login username, and if enabled, your default or login Community and Locale as user session properties. Rhythmyx displays these user session properties and your Role at the top of Content Explorer.

The default Locale is always US English. Your login Locale only displays at the top of Content Explorer with the other user session properties if you have more than one Locale. You can click on your Community or Locale to change it for the active session.

Your session properties determine your access to Content Items and Action Menu options and which parts of Content Explorer and Site Explorer you can view. In addition Rhythmyx records your session properties as metadata of Content Items that you create and transition.
