Accessing a URL Link

The Copy URL to Clipboard option on the Action Menu lets users copy a Content Item's URL and paste it as a link into another application that has access to Rhythmyx, such as Microsoft Outlook or Lotus Notes. Rhythmyx may also send a Content Item URL link in an email message sent during a Workflow Transition.

If you are logged in to a third-party application that includes a Content Item URL link, you may click on the link to access the Content Item.

To access a Rhythmyx Content Item through a link in a third-party application:

  1. Click on the link.

    If you have not yet logged in to Rhythmyx, a dialog prompts you for your username and password. Rhythmyx logs you in with the same Community as the Content Item, or your default Community, if you are not a member of the Content Item's Community.

  2. If the Content Item is in multiple Sites or multiple Folders, a screen prompts you to choose the Site, Folder, or the combination of Site and Folder:

  3. After you choose one of the choices in the Choose item location screen (or if no screen appears), an Action panel with Content Item metadata and Action Menu options opens. Clickable options appear underlined when your cursor is over them.

  4. Click on an Action Menu option to perform the action on the Content Item.
