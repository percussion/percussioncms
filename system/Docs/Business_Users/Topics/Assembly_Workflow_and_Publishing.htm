Assembly, Workflow, and Publishing

Workflow is an integral part of the Rhythmyx Content Management System. All Content Items exist in one of several Workflow States. At least one of these States must indicate that the Content Item is eligible to be published (is a public State). In general, only content items in a public State are eligible to be extracted from the database, and thus are available to be assembled.

Assemblers are invoked by the Rhythmyx Publishing System. Publishing activates the content list applications that extract content items from the Repository, then calls the assembly applications to merge the content items with the stylesheets to produce the final output.
