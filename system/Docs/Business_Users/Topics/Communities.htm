Communities

A Community represents a group of roles that require access to similar information in Rhythmyx. A role can be associated with more than one Community, and users belong to all of the Communities associated with their roles.  

Communities are optional in Rhythmyx. If the Communities feature is enabled, Rhythmyx only gives you access to the Content Editors, Workflows, user interface components, sites, and Templates associated with your Community. If Communities are disabled, you have access to all Content Editors, workflows, user interface components, sites, and Templates.

If the Communities feature is enabled, you must log in under one of your Communities. Rhythmyx automatically selects your community if:

  • you have a default Community;
  • you have only one Community;
  • you chose a Community for the previous login (Rhythmyx logs you in under the same Community).

Otherwise, Rhythmyx prompts you to choose a login Community.

A Content Item has the Community of the user who creates it.
