Editing Word in Rhythmyx

You must enter a Microsoft Word document to enter the name, author, abstract, and body of a Word-based content item. The Word-based Content Editor provides a Launch Word link that opens a Microsoft Word document. In the Word document, you have access to most Microsoft Word features and to other features that let you interact with Rhythmyx.

You may access additional Help on editing Word in Rhythmyx after you enter the Word document.
