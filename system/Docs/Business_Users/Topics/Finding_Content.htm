Finding Content

Content Explorer provides you with several methods for locating specific Content Items or categories of Content Items. If your give your folders meaningful names and if they represent narrow enough categories, you should be able to find many the Content Items that you have created by navigating through them. Use searches or views to find content that is ready for you to review and to select and sort content by its properties.
