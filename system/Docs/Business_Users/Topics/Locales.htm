Locales

Your Locale represents the language and the dialect of the language associated with your login and determines the language displayed on your interface. The default Locale is always US English.

Rhythmyx automatically logs you on with a Locale if:

  • US English is your only Locale;
  • you chose a Locale for the previous login (Rhythmyx logs you in under the same Locale).

Otherwise, you must choose a Locale when you log on.

Content Explorer displays your Locale with your User Session Properties if you have more than one Locale.

See Choosing a Language when Logging in to Rhythmyx for more information about Locales.
