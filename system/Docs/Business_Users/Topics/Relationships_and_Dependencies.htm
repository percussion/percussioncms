Relationships and Dependencies

Content items with a Relationship are associated with one another. In each Relationship, the original Content Item is the owner and the attached or duplicated Content Item is its dependency.

The following are the default Relationship types in Rhythmyx:

Relationship

Owner/Dependency

Default Characteristics

Related Content

Content Item/Related Content Item in one of its Slots

The two items move through their Workflows independently.

 

 

Inline Links

Content Item/Content Item referenced by link

The two items move through their Workflows independently.

 

 

Copies

 

Content Item/New Copy of Content Item

The new copy has the same dependencies as the original Content Item. The original item and the new copy move through their Workflows independently.

 

Promotable Versions

Content Item/New Version of Content Item

The new Version has the same dependencies as the original Content Item. When the Version reaches the public State, Rhythmyx transitions the original Content Item into the archive State.

Translation Copies

 

Content Item/Translation Copy

When a Translation Copy is created, Translation Copies for all of the original Content Item's dependencies are also created and become dependencies of the Translation Copy. The original item and the Translation Copy move through their Workflows independently.

 

Each Relationship type has a different function in Rhythmyx. For more information about the default Relationship types, click on the Relationship name, above.

In your CMS, the default behavior of Relationships may be different, and additional, custom Relationships may exist.
