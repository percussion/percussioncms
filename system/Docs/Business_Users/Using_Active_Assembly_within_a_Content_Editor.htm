Performing Active Assembly in Expert Mode

Content Editors offer content contributors an Expert Mode interface.

The text-only interface displays the available Slots for a Content Type and allows users to add, remove, and shuffle content items within the Slot. It also lets them change the Templates used to format the content items.

When using Expert Mode, it is important to realize that all Slots displayed may not reside on every Template registered to the Content Type. Be sure to have a good understanding of the Slot-Template relationships for the Content Type being edited.

To perform Active Assembly in Expert Mode:

  1. Access the content item to which you want to add related content and open it in a content editor.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page to the Related Content box.

  3. Click the [Edit All] button.

    An Editor Assembly window opens:

  4. Click on a related content Slot(ID) link.
  5. Rhythmyx displays the Content Browser (Note: Your system may be configured to display a regular search dialog instead).
  6. Browse for or search for the content item you want to assign to the Slot.

    Once you add an item, the Content Browser closes.

  7. Change the positions of items in Slots, move them to different Slots, or change their Templates.
