Find and Replace

Use the Find and Replace dialog to find text you specify and replace it with new text you specify. To access the Find and Replace dialog,

choose Edit > Replace; or

press CTRL-R.

Enter the text you want to replace in the Find What field, and enter the new text in the Replace what field.

Click [Next] to go to the next occurrence of the text you specified in Find what.

Click [Replace] to replace the currently selected text and then find the next occurrence of the text.

Click [All] to find and replace all occurrences of Find what with Replace what.

Check Match case to make your find and replace case-sensitive..

To close the dialog,

click [Cancel]; or

press <Escape>.
